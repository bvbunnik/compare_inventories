COMPARE INVENTORIES DATABASE

The COMPARE inventories database holds a variety of collections containing different types of information.
Currently we have a very large inventory of existing sampling and storage protocols, a catalogue with tools
for rapid assessment of key transmission parameters and rate of spread estimates
and an inventory of pipelines to be used with Next Generation Sequencing data.
